Equipment Qualification (IQ/OQ/PQ) Essentials

Learn how to plan, execute, and document qualification activities with templates and examples.

Overview

Equipment qualification is a critical part of Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) and ensures that equipment is fit for its intended purpose. The process typically includes:

  • Design Qualification (DQ): Verifying that design meets user requirements and regulatory standards.
  • Installation Qualification (IQ): Confirming equipment is installed correctly and according to specifications.
  • Operational Qualification (OQ): Testing that equipment operates as intended throughout all operating ranges.
  • Performance Qualification (PQ): Demonstrating equipment performs effectively and reproducibly under real conditions.

Key Regulatory References

  • EU GMP Annex 15 - Qualification and Validation
  • WHO Technical Report Series, No. 937, Annex 4
