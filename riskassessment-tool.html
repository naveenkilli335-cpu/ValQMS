ICH Q9 Risk Assessment Tools

📋 Risk Management Tools (ICH Q9(R1))

Available Tools: FMEA, HACCP, HAZOP, Risk Ranking, Preliminary Hazard Analysis

RPN Calculation: Severity × Occurrence × Detection (Scale 1-10 each)

Risk Levels: Critical (RPN ≥200), High (100-199), Medium (40-99), Low (<40)

Severity (S)
10: Hazardous - Patient safety risk
8-9: Major - Product quality impact
5-7: Moderate - Minor quality impact
3-4: Minor - Detectable deviation
1-2: Negligible - No significant impact
Occurrence (O)
10: Very High - Almost inevitable
8-9: High - Frequent failures
5-7: Moderate - Occasional failures
3-4: Low - Rare failures
1-2: Remote - Highly unlikely
Detection (D)
10: Cannot detect - No controls
8-9: Very Low - Minimal detection
5-7: Moderate - Some controls exist
3-4: High - Good detection methods
1-2: Very High - Almost certain detection
